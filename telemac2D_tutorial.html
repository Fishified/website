Telemac2D tutorial

Hello! I have just posted a series of videos to Youtube explaining how to use Telemac2D for modeling 2D hydrodynamic flows. The tutorials are based on the paper tutorials that were produced by the kind people at Oregon State University (thank you Christopher Gifford-Miears and Arturo S. Leon).

Though the tutorial files are available for download on the Telemac website, I have made a number of small changes to a number of them. If you want to follow along exactly, you can download the files here:
