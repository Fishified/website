Welcome!

I am a researcher with an interest in pretty much everything related to fluid mechanics, hydraulics, rivers, sediment transport and ecohydraulics. Topics like computational fluid dynamics (CFD), advanced experimental flow visualization techniques (sPIV), object tracking in 3-dimensions using computer vision techniques, Python GUI application development to build research tools and Structure from Motion photogrammetry are what are currently attracting my attention.

Feel free to browse my new site (excuse the mess, it is still very much under construction) to get a better feel of my research interests and to learn what I am currently up to.

If you are interested in having me model an ecohydraulic structure for fish passage purposes or apply a 2D model to gain insights on the hydraulics of a riverian system please contact me.
